Bicycle Thief

Gin
Campari
Bitter
Citrus
  • 1 oz Gin
  • 1 oz Campari
  • 1½ oz Grapefruit Juice
  • ½ oz Lemon Juice
  • ½ oz Simple Syrup
  • Top with Soda
  • Garnish with a grapefuit slice

From Zachary Glenaw-Rubin and Abraham Hawkins at Dutch Kills in Long Island City, the Bicycle Thief is a bittersweet gin cocktail. Best served as an apéritif, this drink exhibits the bitterness of Campari and grapefruit, finishing with a hint of sweet citrus. Shake and double strain into a collins glass, top with soda, and garnish with a slice of grapefruit.
